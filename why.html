Why Clay?

  • Natural Alkaline: Balances food acidity, making meals healthier.
  • Moisture Retention: Keeps food juicy and soft without requiring extra oil.
  • Earth Friendly: 100% biodegradable and sustainable.

The Artisan's Touch

Our clay is hand-picked from the Indus riverbanks, sun-dried, and fire-baked in traditional kilns. No chemicals, just earth. It represents centuries of tradition passed down through generations.

A History of Earth

Pottery is one of the oldest human inventions, originating before the Neolithic period. From storing grains to cooling water, clay vessels have been an integral part of civilization. The Indus Valley Civilization, one of the world's oldest, was renowned for its advanced pottery techniques.
